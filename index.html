COVID-19 the number 1 reason for WARN notices

The virus has lead to over 1000 notices in the past 3 months.
By Daisy Williams
Published May. 6, 2020

According to the Department of Labor The Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act (WARN) protects workers, their families, and communities by requiring employers with 100 or more employees to provide at least 60 calendar days advance written notice of a plant closing and mass layoff affecting 50 or more employees at a single site of employment.
